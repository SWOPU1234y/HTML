Welcome to Chittagong Grammar School, where tradition meets innovation.

Chittagong Grammar School, founded in 1992, expanded its reach to Dhaka in 2010 with the establishment of Chittagong Grammar School Dhaka. Established with a vision to provide world-class education, our school stands as a beacon of academic excellence, nurturing young minds to become confident, capable, and compassionate global citizens. Join us at Chittagong Grammar School, where we shape the leaders of tomorrow. Discover a place where every child's journey is celebrated and nurtured. Welcome to the future of education.
